"Compassionate, evidence-based pediatric care — from the first breath to every milestone that follows."

I am a pediatrician trained at Chettinad Hospital & Research Institute, Chennai, with an MD in Pediatrics and hands-on experience across NICU, PICU, OPD, and inpatient wards. My practice is rooted in a deep commitment to patient-centered care and evidence-based medicine.

My special interests include neonatal resuscitation, pediatric nutrition, and preventive child healthcare. I believe in empowering parents through counseling and education, supporting every step of their child's development from infancy through adolescence.
